What is Oscillate?

Oscillate is a web application that allows users to create visual and audio representations of musical notes. By typing in a sequence of notes, users can see waveforms drawn on a canvas and hear the corresponding sounds.

How to Use

  1. Type Notes: Enter notes (e.g., C D E F G) in the input field and click Submit to play and visualize them. You can use sharps (#), flats (b), and octaves (e.g., C#5, G3).
  2. Customize the Visuals: Change line thickness, waveform type, gradient direction, and colors using the controls below the canvas.
  3. Audio Settings: Adjust the volume and speed sliders to set how the notes sound and how fast the art animates.
  4. Random Colors: Check the Random Colors box to use random colors for your wave gradients.
  5. Background: Pick a background color for the canvas for a different mood.
  6. Record & Download: Click Start Recording to record both your art and its music as a webm video file. Click Stop Recording to automatically download your creation!

Tips

  • Try different waveforms for unique sounds and visuals.
  • Use both colors and gradients for more dynamic artwork.
  • Try different waveform types (sine, square, sawtooth, triangle) to see how they affect the sound and look of your art.
  • Mix notes and octaves to produce a melody you love.
  • Adjust the speed slider to control how quickly the waveforms animate across the canvas.
  • Your downloaded webm file is great for sharing!
